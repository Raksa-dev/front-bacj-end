Our Philosophy

At Raksa, our philosophy is shaped by the wisdom of great thinkers and timeless principles. We believe in integrating these philosophies into our astrological services to provide a holistic approach to understanding and navigating life.

Ashtavakra: Liberation from Illusion

Ashtavakra’s teachings focus on breaking free from illusions and achieving true self-awareness. Raksa aims to help you see beyond surface-level appearances and understand deeper truths, guiding you toward liberation from misconceptions and false beliefs.

Vedanta: Unity and Knowledge

Vedanta teaches the oneness of all existence and the importance of self-knowledge. At Raksa, we integrate these teachings to help you realize your connection to the universe and your true nature. Our guidance aims to deepen your understanding and promote a sense of unity and inner peace.

Socrates: "Know Thyself"

Socrates taught us the importance of self-awareness and introspection. At Raksa, we encourage you to delve deep into your own nature and understand your true self. Our personalized astrological insights are designed to help you achieve greater self-awareness and make informed decisions.

Buddha: Mindfulness and Compassion

Buddha's teachings emphasize mindfulness and compassion. We incorporate these principles to help you cultivate a mindful approach to life’s challenges. Our insights aim to promote inner peace and empathy, guiding you to navigate life’s ups and downs with a compassionate heart.

Lao Tzu: Harmony and Simplicity

Lao Tzu’s philosophy centers on living in harmony with the natural flow of life and embracing simplicity. Raksa’s approach encourages you to align with life’s natural rhythms and simplify your journey, helping you find balance and ease amidst the complexities of life.
